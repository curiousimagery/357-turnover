## Closeout checklist

### On arrival

- **Start a load of laundry** — Smell and inspect the living-room throw blankets and pillow covers; if they need it, run a quick cold wash and dry on low. If they're already clean, start the towels (including kitchen towels) on hot.
- **Air out the space** — Open the windows to air things out, weather permitting.
- **Charge the cabinet lights** — Plug the under-cabinet lights in to charge (on the living-room side table).

### Kitchen

- **Dishes** — Wash dishes and unload the dishwasher; leave it open to dry. _Helper: Check that everything was put away clean — silverware, dishes, glasses, pots and pans._
- **Coffee grinder** — Clear and clean the grinder, then compost the grounds. _Helper: Tap the button/lever on the right repeatedly until grounds clear from the top; compost them, rinse and dry the cup, and wipe up loose grounds. Keep water and cleaner out of the grinder itself._
- **Electric kettle** — Make sure it looks and smells clean; air-dry it upside down if wet, then set it back on its base.
- **Drying rack** — Clean the dish drying rack if needed.
- **Oven & stove** — Clean the oven and stovetop.
- **Handles & knobs** — Wipe the cabinet handles, stove knobs, and fridge/freezer handles.
- **Fridge & freezer** — Check both; clean if needed and compost any perishables left behind.
- **Counters & island** — Wipe the counters and the island — top, sides, shelves, and underneath.
- **Trash & sorting** — Empty the trash and replace the liners. _Helper: Guests don't always sort well — check the bags and move items into recycling/compost/landfill as needed, then wipe the inside of the recycling and compost bins._
- **Kitchen inventory** — Check the kitchen refills (see the inventory list).

### Living area

- **Blankets & pillows** — Tidy the blankets and pillows. _Helper: If they're in the wash, don't forget to put them back._
- **Dust** — Dust the white plant shelf and the windowsills.
- **Tables** — Wipe the coffee and side tables.
- **Electronics** — Dry-dust the lamps, TV, and sound bar.

### Bedrooms

- **Bedding** — Strip the beds and remake them with clean linens.
- **Surfaces** — Wipe all surfaces — windowsills, closets, tables, shelves.

### Bathroom

- **Shower bottles** — Wipe soap residue off the bottles and pumps. _Helper: If a bottle is under ⅓ full, swap in a fresh one and leave the low one on the airlock shelf — don't toss it._
- **Drain** — Clean the drain plug and hair catcher.
- **Shower & tub** — Clean the walls, tub, and window shelf, plus the grab bar, knobs, and sprayer. _Helper: Spray vinegar in the shower to help prevent pink bacteria._
- **Toilet** — Clean the toilet. _Helper: Double-check for hair or drips under and around the sides, including the floor and walls._
- **Towels** — Wipe the towel shelves; restock clean towels and swap out used ones. _Helper: Trifold the folded towels._
- **Sink & counter** — Clean the countertop and sink.
- **Hand soap** — Wipe the bottle; replace if under ¼ full (kitchen and bath). _Helper: Leave the low bottle on the airlock shelf — don't throw it away._
- **Cabinets** — Wipe the handles (fronts/sides if needed); check inside for left items and wipe if needed. _Helper: Basic shared items are fine to leave._
- **Mirror** — Clean the mirror and wipe the shelves if needed.
- **Trash** — Empty the trash and dust off the lid.

### Laundry room

- **Lint trap** — Clean the dryer lint trap. _Helper: A vacuum is easiest._
- **Washer & dryer** — Wipe the inside and outside if needed.

### General (every visit)

- **Inventory** — Double-check all refills (see the inventory list).
- **Plants** — Water only if the soil is dry about 1" down (copper can on the white shelf). _Helper: Flag any plants looking sad from low light._
- **Mirrors** — Clean the kitchen and bedroom mirrors.
- **Vacuum** — Vacuum everywhere. _Helper: Don't forget the door mats and the dust bunnies under the beds._
- **Mop** — Mop all the hard flooring.

### Deep clean (rotate — aim for at least one each visit)

_Helper for the group: Pick at least one of these each visit so they each get done about once a month._

- **Air purifier filter** — Check the front filter; if it's coated in dust, vacuum it with the Dyson brush attachment (or rinse and fully dry before replacing). _Helper: Hold the reset button after cleaning. Let me know if the charcoal filter behind it is getting dirty. Keeping up with this means less dusting for you._
- **Baseboards** — Wipe the baseboards. _Helper: Don't forget behind the beds and beside the washer/dryer._
- **Detailed dusting** — Dyson long-neck + brush attachment is easiest (about monthly); damp-cloth wipe about every other month.
- **Windows** — Clean the windows.
- **Couches** — Vacuum with the handheld Dyson attachment.
- **Stove vent** — Clean the vent — the bottom and the top.
- **Bathroom vent** — Clean the vent (Dyson long-neck + brush, or a damp cloth).
- **Microwave** — Wipe inside and out (it's kept on the shelf).
- **High dusting** — Above door frames, on top of the fridge, ceiling fixtures. _Helper: Step stool is by the backup-supply shelf._

### Before you leave

- **Laundry** — Check the washer and dryer (and lint trap) for finished or unfinished loads; pack up anything to be washed off-site.
- **Cabinet lights** — Put the under-cabinet lights back in place. _Helper: Make sure the switch is set to "G."_
- **Test the lamps** — Use the remotes (living area + each bedroom) to turn every lamp on and off, including the salt lamps. _Helper: If a light doesn't respond, find its power switch and turn it on, then turn it off again with the remote before you leave._
- **Coffee** — Set out a fresh bag of coffee for the next guest. _Helper: If a guest left an open bag, leave it for me on the airlock shelf._
- **Flowers** — Replace the fresh flowers by the coffee if any are available in the garden.
- **Windows** — Set the windows for the next guest. _Helper: Same-day check-in and 60–75°F with no rain → leave open to keep airing out. Below ~55°F → close them and leave a space heater on in the living room set to 70°F. No one coming same day → close them._
- **Power down** — Turn everything off except the air purifier (turn it on if it's off).
- **Outside** — Tidy the exterior entry: pick up any garbage and check if anything needs attention.
- **Text Daniel** — Let me know how clean the guest left it, anything broken, and any low/missing inventory.
